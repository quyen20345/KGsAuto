⭐️ PROBABLY THE BEST PLACE TO START YOUR CAREER ⭐️

Having the spirit of “SOMETHING STRONGER’S BREWING”, Carlsberg Vietnam Graduate Trainee Program – Growing Future Leaders aims to bring great opportunities for young people to build their own a stronger and better version not only in the local country but also on a global scale – “LOCAL AMBITION, GLOBAL REACH”.

– Opportunity to rotate across departments (Function Rotation) to accumulate knowledge and practical experience on the business overview
