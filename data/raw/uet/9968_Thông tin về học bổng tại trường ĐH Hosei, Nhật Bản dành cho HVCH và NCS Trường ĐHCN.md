## Content

Hosei University conducts a special examination based on university recommendation for Institute of Integrated Science and Technology (liST).

liST is an English-based graduate program focusing on integrated science and technology. This special examination is only available for specific partner universities and offers earlier application to the students attending the universities than the students who apply for the general examination.

Please note that the applicants must be recommended by a President, a Vicepresident, a dean or equivalent of their last school that they attended or graduated from.
