GC Vietnam is an EPC company in oil & gas. We are now looking for an IT Engineer with job description as following: 1.  Job description: – Implement the operation and maintenance for IT equipment and network system – Perform IT-related maintenance in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City offices and other sites; – Set up and configure IT equipment, install software license and support; – Control PC user accounts, control documents related to IT, IT inventory; – Control security: Network, PC, IT equipment; – Support users daily, help-desk and other IT trouble shootings; – Define and improve all system and network operations processes; – Cooperate with other departments to complete jobs. – Take over other duties as requested by management 2.  Requirements:

– Qualifications: Bachelor Degree in IT related courses (Computer Science, Computer Applications, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Telecommunications, etc.);

– New graduates are preferable – Proficiency English skills; – Certified professionals will be preferred (CCNA, MCSA); – Have knowledge of programming basic concepts as HTML, Java…; – Have knowledge of SQL Server, Oracle; – Be willing to travel on job demand 3. Apply mode: – Interested candidates please submit Cover letter and CV in English to hr@jgcvietnam.vn 4. Benefit: – Professional working environment – Competitive salary package – Chances for training and development – Out-side activities For more information please visit our website: http://jgcvietnamjobs.com/
