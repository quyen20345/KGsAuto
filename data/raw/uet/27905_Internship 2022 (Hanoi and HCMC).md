We are now looking for undergraduate students to join Ericsson Vietnam in a six-month Internship program. This program is designed to support you to gain experience how things operate in an international, technology – leader organization and learn multiple soft skills in communications and leaderships, etc. which will definitely help you in your future professional endeavors.

What you will do Be interactive with different functional teams and its leaders to understand how we work Be part of the team and get chances to train up yourself with communication, negotiation, management skills set Be responsible for your own project which will be delivered by end of the program To be successful in the role you must have: Have desire to learn and be open to change yourself for good thing Be innovative and creative Be accountable and committed with the program Education background from IT, Engineering, Telecommunication, Computer Science Why join Ericsson?
